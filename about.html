About Dr. Vinod Kumar Jangir

Dr. Vinod Kumar Jangir is a highly qualified M.D. Physician and Diabetologist holding a Fellowship in Diabetes Mellitus (FIDM). He founded Shri Vishwakarma Clinic with a mission to make advanced medical care accessible to all sections of society. His clinic offers services such as Diabetes Care, Sonology, General Medicine, and Comprehensive Diagnostics under one roof.

Known for his calm demeanor and patient-first approach, Dr. Jangir has helped hundreds of patients achieve better health through timely diagnosis and lifestyle management.
