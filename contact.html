Clinic Overview

At Shri Vishwakarma Clinic, we combine compassionate care with advanced medical technology. Under the guidance of Dr. Vinod Kumar Jangir, we provide complete health diagnostics, diabetes management, ultrasound services, and preventive healthcare — all in one trusted center.

Our clinic is equipped with X-ray, ECG, and advanced ultrasound systems. We aim to ensure early diagnosis, accurate treatment, and better outcomes for every patient who visits us.
